Getting Started
How do I create an account?expand_more
Tap "Create Account" on the login screen. Enter your email and password (6+ characters). Check your email for a verification link — click it, then sign in. You can also sign in instantly with Google.
What exams does EXAMORA cover?expand_more
EXAMORA covers JAMB UTME, WAEC SSCE, NECO SSCE, Post-UTME, and many subject-specific tests. The AI tutor NOVA also helps with SAT, IELTS, GRE, A-Levels, and professional certifications.
How do I start a practice exam?expand_more
Tap "Exam" on the bottom navigation. Select your exam type (e.g. JAMB), subject, difficulty, and number of questions. Tap "Start Exam" and begin! After finishing, you'll see a detailed result breakdown.
AI Tutor (NOVA)
Who is NOVA?expand_more
NOVA is EXAMORA's AI study companion. She can explain any concept, solve maths step-by-step, give exam hints, generate practice questions, build your study plan, and check your essay answers. Access NOVA from the chat icon on any screen.
Why is NOVA slow sometimes?expand_more
NOVA uses free AI models via the internet. Response time depends on your connection speed and server load. Free users share server capacity — Premium users get faster responses. Try refreshing if NOVA takes more than 30 seconds.
How do I use the Problem Solver?expand_more
Go to Home → Problem Solver. Type any maths, physics, or chemistry question. NOVA will show a full step-by-step solution. You can save solutions to your Notes or ask NOVA follow-up questions in chat.
Premium & Billing
What does Premium include?expand_more
Premium gives you: unlimited AI sessions with NOVA, all 60,000+ past questions, full offline mode, unlimited mock exams, advanced analytics, AI flashcard generation, essay checking, step-by-step solver, and priority support.
Can I get a free trial?expand_more
Yes! New users get a 7-day free trial with full Premium access — no payment card required. Go to the Upgrade page and tap "Start Free Trial."
How do I cancel my subscription?expand_more
Email us at billing@examora.com.ng with your account email and we'll cancel your subscription. You keep Premium access until the end of your current billing period.
Do you offer refunds?expand_more
Yes. We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for new Premium subscribers. If you're not satisfied, email billing@examora.com.ng within 7 days of your first payment and we'll refund you in full.
How do I apply a promo code?expand_more
On the Upgrade page, scroll down to the price card. You'll see a "Promo code" field below the payment button. Enter your code and tap Apply. Valid codes include JAMB2025 (30% off) and WAEC50 (50% off).
Account & Data
How do I reset my password?expand_more
On the login screen, tap "Forgot password?" Enter your email address and we'll send a password reset link. Check your spam folder if you don't see it within a few minutes.
How do I change my profile photo?expand_more
Go to your Profile page. Tap your avatar photo — a crop tool will appear. Choose and position your photo, then tap "Use Photo." It will upload automatically to your account.
How does offline mode work?expand_more
Install EXAMORA as a PWA (tap "Add to Home Screen" in your browser). Premium users can access previously loaded questions and their saved notes, flashcards, and study plan offline. AI features require internet.
How do I delete my account?expand_more
Go to Profile → scroll to the bottom → tap "Delete Account." Confirm the action. All your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days. This action cannot be undone.
School Mode
How do I join my teacher's class?expand_more
Go to Home → School Mode. Tap "Join Class" and enter the 6-digit code your teacher gave you. Once joined, you can see class assignments and your ranking among classmates.
How do I set up a class as a teacher?expand_more
Contact us at support@examora.com.ng to get teacher access. Once enabled, go to School Mode — a class is created automatically with a unique 6-digit code to share with students.
